# Video Demo Script (45-60 seconds) - MM3772

## 1) Short Video Plan (45-60 seconds)
- **0-10s:** Show FPGA board, VGA monitor, and a visible label/card reading `MM3772`.
- **10-25s:** Show programmed design running on VGA (playfield and active block visible).
- **25-45s:** Demonstrate left/right/down/reset button inputs with immediate on-screen response.
- **45-60s:** End with stable gameplay frame including `MM3772` marker and brief verbal close identifying evidence purpose.

## 2) What to Show on the FPGA Board
- Physical board powered on.
- USB programming connection (if visible).
- Hand pressing mapped push buttons used for movement/reset.

## 3) What to Show on the VGA Monitor
- Playfield border/grid output.
- Active falling block movement.
- `MM3772` on-screen marker for identity traceability.

## 4) Which Buttons to Press
- Press **left** once: show one-step left movement.
- Press **right** once: show one-step right movement.
- Press **down** once or twice: show faster descent.
- Press **reset** once: show clean state reset.

## 5) How to Make MM3772 Visible in Evidence
- Keep `MM3772` visible on the VGA output during at least one continuous shot.
- Also include a printed/written `MM3772` card in the camera frame near the FPGA board.
- Name exported video file with MM3772 prefix (example: `MM3772_final_hardware_demo.mp4`).
